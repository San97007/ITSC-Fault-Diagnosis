## Exception: `table_bh_curve_50W470.csv`

The B-H curve data points for grade 50W470 silicon steel are derived from
the **IEC 60404-2 standard** ("Magnetic materials - Part 2: Methods of
measurement of the magnetic properties of electrical steel sheet and
strip by means of an Epstein frame"). IEC standards are copyrighted by the
International Electrotechnical Commission and may not be freely
redistributed in full. The nine data points included here are a small,
transformative excerpt used solely to document the material model inputs
to the digital twin simulation, consistent with the manuscript's citation
of this standard. Readers requiring the complete standard should obtain
it directly from IEC (https://webstore.iec.ch/).
